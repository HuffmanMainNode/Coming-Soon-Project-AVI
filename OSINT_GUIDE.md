# 🧠 Maximizing OSINT & Avoiding Digital Scams

### 1. The AVI OSINT Mindset
*   **Data Aggregation:** Use tools like `Curated-Modern-OSINT-Tools` to build a footprint before you engage.
*   **Signal vs. Noise:** If a project promises decentralization but holds the keys, it's a trap.

### 2. Scam Prevention
*   Watch for 'Energy Vampires'—scripts that consume high resources for low output (often hidden miners).
